Welcome to Pane!

A modern clipboard manager that lives in your system tray.

WHAT IT DOES:
- Automatically saves everything you copy (text, images, code, links, colors)
- Categorizes your clips automatically
- Search instantly across your clipboard history
- Organize clips into collections

HOW TO USE:
- Press Ctrl+Shift+V anytime to open Pane
- Click any clip to copy it again
- Press Escape to dismiss
- Pane runs quietly in your system tray

Pane is free software. If you find it useful, consider supporting development at ko-fi.com/juanylato

By installing, you agree that Pane stores your clipboard data locally on your computer. No data is sent to any server. Your clipboard history is 100% private.
